From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a list of countries by obesity rate, with data from the World Health Organization (WHO), as of 2022. There are 191 registered countries on this ranking. This excludes Monaco, Liechtenstein, Vatican City and San Marino.

This list is dubious, as it differs significantly from the reference given, with unlikely changes from 2022.
