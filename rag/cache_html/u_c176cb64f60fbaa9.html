Fastest tortoise

Share

Contact an Account Manager
Apply Now
Who
Bertie
What
0.28 metre(s) per second
Where
United Kingdom (Brasside)

The greatest speed achieved by a tortoise is 0.28 m/s (0.92 ft/s) and was achieved by Bertie at Adventure Valley, Brasside, UK on 9 July 2014.

Bertie ran the speed over a 5.48m course set at a gradient of 1:12 in a time of 19.59 seconds

Records change on a daily basis and are not immediately published online. For a full list of record titles, please use our Record Application Search. (You will need to register / login for access)
